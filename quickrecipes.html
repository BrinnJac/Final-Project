Sweet Tooth Cravings

Apple Crisp

Cuisine: American

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Serving Serving: Single

Calories: 713 cals

Author: @essential_everyday


Ingredient

Filling

1 apple, peeled & diced

1 tsbp butter, cut into small cubes

2 tbsp brown sugar

1 tsp cornstarch

1/2 tsp cinnamon

Topping

2 tbsp butter, softened

2 tbsp brown sugar

2 tbsp Essential Everyday Quick 1 Minute Oats

2 tbsp flour

1/2 tsp cinnamon

Optional

Add a scoop of ice cream

Add Caramel drizzle
