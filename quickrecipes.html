Bacon Lettuce Tomato Sandwich (Developer Edition)

Cuisine: American

Cook Time:15 minutes

Serving Size: 2

Calories: 1075 cals

Author: Jackson Brinn


Ingredients

4 slices of StoneHouse Bread Asiago Sourdough

2 thin slices of tomato

Bag shredded lettuce

Bottle of mayo

Butter


Instructions

Turn the stove to medium high heat.

Butter both sides of the bread and then place in pan.

Done when golden brown.

Place the bacon in pan and cook to preference.

While bacon is cooking, spread a light layer of mayo on one side of the bread on all four slices.

Slice the tomato and place on the mayo side of bread and add lettuce.

When bacon is done, add the bacon to the sandwich. Enjoy!

Grilled Cheese

Cuisine: American

Cook Time: 15 mins

Serving Size: 2

Calories: 400

Author: Sara


Ingredients

4 slices white bread

3 tbsp butter

2 slices of cheddar cheese


Instructions

Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.

Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.

Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.

Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.

Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.

Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
